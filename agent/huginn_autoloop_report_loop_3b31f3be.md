The accurate prediction of the silicon band gap remains a fundamental challenge for density functional theory (DFT). Standard functionals—LDA and GGA—are known to systematically underestimate the experimental band gap of Si (1.12 eV at 300 K) by 30–50% [2]. This study investigates whether the choice of functional (LDA vs. GGA) influences not only the static band gap but also its temperature dependence, a critical parameter for device applications.
