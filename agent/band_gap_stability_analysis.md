# Band Gap vs Stability: Symbolic Analysis and Research Direction

## Symbolic Analysis via Bourbaki

The dimensional analysis confirms that **band gap (E_g in eV)** and **formation energy (ΔH_f in eV)** share the same energy dimension, making their direct correlation physically meaningful. The key relationships are:

### 1. Phillips-Van Vechten Gap Decomposition
E_g² = V² + C²

where:
- V = ionic gap (from electronegativity difference Δχ)
- C = covalent gap (from bond hybridization)

The **ionicity fraction** f_i = V²/(V² + C²) determines both the band gap character and the thermodynamic stability trend.

### 2. Stability Window = E_g
For intrinsic semiconductors, the Fermi level E_F is constrained to:
E_v < E_F < E_c

This means the **chemical potential window** for stable doping is exactly E_g wide.

### 3. Defect Formation Energy
E_f^def(q) = E_f^def(0) + q · E_F

Since E_F ∈ [0, E_g], the maximum variation in defect formation energy is |q| × E_g.

### 4. Correlation Model
The hypothesized form is: ΔH_f ≈ α · E_g + β

where α ≈ f_i (ionicity) determines how strongly stability correlates with band gap.
